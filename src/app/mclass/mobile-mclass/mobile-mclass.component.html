Music genre classification


This is a simple tool trying to guess the genre of a provided song or audio fragment. Based on the Mel Spectogram of the provided audio sample, a Convolutional Neural Network is used, providing you with the music genre(s) that most closely resemble the provided audio fragment.

You can provide an audio file, or make a sound recording. In either case, make sure that the audio fragment contains at least 40 seconds of sound, in order to have a higher accuracy obtained by the neural network. Also, please note that a provided audio file will most likely produce more accurate results, as it usually contains more data than a recorded audio sample, and it doesn't include any background noise.

The algorithm chooses between 10 music genres: blues, classical, country, disco, hiphop, jazz, metal, pop, reggae and rock.
